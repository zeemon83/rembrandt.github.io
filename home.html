About Us

Founded on over 15 years of industrial rope access experience, Highline Rope Access deliver a safe and efficient solution to working at height. Our technicians have experience managing a variety of projects ranging from 1 day inspections to ongoing maintenance contracts. We pride ourselves on attention to detail and lateral thinking, constantly providing our customers with an effective solution to any work at height situation.
